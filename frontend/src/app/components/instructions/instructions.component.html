Best Practices Image Guidelines
Before dismissing the patient...
1. Take 30 seconds to scroll through the volume to verify that the area of interest is within the scan
2. Make sure that no significant motion (double or overlapped contours) is within the area of interest (Note that even motion of 1-2mm will affect measurements and evaluation of pathology or an implant site.)
